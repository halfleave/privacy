# Privacy Policy for DoLDoL

Last updated: February 26, 2026

DoLDoL is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding your data.

## 1. Local Storage Only
DoLDoL is a local-only application. All your records, partner information, and media data are stored exclusively on your device. We do not operate any servers and cannot access, collect, or store your private data.

## 2. No Data Collection
We do not collect any personal information, usage data, or tracking information. Your privacy is our top priority.
